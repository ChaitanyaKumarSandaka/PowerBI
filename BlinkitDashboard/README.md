# 📊 Blinkit Sales Analysis (Power BI Project)

---

## 🔹 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to identify key insights and opportunities for optimization using Power BI.

---

## 🎯 KPIs

* **Total Sales** – Overall revenue generated from all items sold
* **Average Sales** – Revenue per transaction
* **Number of Items** – Total items sold
* **Average Rating** – Customer satisfaction metric

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ Total Sales by Fat Content

* Analyze how fat content impacts total sales
* Compare KPIs across fat categories

### 2️⃣ Total Sales by Item Type

* Identify best-performing item categories
* Compare performance across KPIs

### 3️⃣ Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales

* Compare outlet-wise sales segmented by fat content
* Evaluate KPI variations across outlets

### 4️⃣ Total Sales by Outlet Establishment

* Analyze how outlet type or age affects sales performance

### 5️⃣ Sales by Outlet Size

* Understand relationship between outlet size and revenue contribution

### 6️⃣ Sales by Outlet Location

* Analyze geographic distribution of sales

### 7️⃣ Metrics by Outlet Type

Compare all KPIs:

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items
* Average Rating
